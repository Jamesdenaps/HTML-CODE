Floyd Mayweather Jr.

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is a retired professional boxer widely considered one of the greatest boxers of all time. He was born on February 24, 1977, in Grand Rapids, Michigan, USA. He also made the most money in boxing history.

Mayweather won multiple world titles in different weight classes and retired with an undefeated record. His defensive skills, speed, and ring intelligence made him a dominant force in the sport.
